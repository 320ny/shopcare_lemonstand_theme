Many of the abundant plants that grow along the roadsides and in the fields of the Burren are in fact medicinal or cosmetic herbs which have many uses long since forgotten. The garden was laid out to reflect those different usages.

Cold Soap Making

The soaps we make at the Burren Perfumery are mainly made using what is known as the cold process.

Cold process soap is made by mixing oils and lye (sodium hydroxide) at a certain temperature such that the (acid) fats chemically combine with the (base) lye and saponify into soup (a salt).
